About Us

We started as a small family-run bakery in Seattle, Wa on 1st and Pike. We have grown to five locations around Seattle. We use locally-sourced salmon in our cookies and all organic ingredients. The salmon is finely ground and added to the flour so you get all the protein but not a strong fishy taste!
